Method

Interpretation

Mouse and rat sequences show high similarity.
Bovine sequence exhibits minor variations.
Conserved regions suggest functional importance in oxygen transport and heme binding.
Presence of many * symbols indicates strong evolutionary conservation.

Conclusion
The MSA revealed highly conserved amino acid residues among mammalian hemoglobin beta proteins, demonstrating their evolutionary relationship and functional significance.
